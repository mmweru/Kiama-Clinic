Inkless stretch marks and scars tattoo camouflage(MCA Needling)

  • This method invloves using tattoo machines and serums to carefully penetrate the skin and treat areas that need repairing. Minimal trauma is initiated to breakdown the scar tissue and stimulate collagen during healing process.
  • Please be aware that tattooing with ink may not be necessary, this will be discussed further during initial consultation
